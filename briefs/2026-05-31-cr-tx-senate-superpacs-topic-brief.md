## The story (RESOLVED, past tense)
Over **$110M in outside (super-PAC) money** poured into the 2026 Texas U.S. Senate race — the heaviest super-PAC spending of the cycle. The twist: the biggest checks **lost**. Pro-Cornyn money spent **$39M against Ken Paxton** to stop him, and **Paxton won** the May 26 GOP runoff anyway (63.8%). On the Democratic side, pro-Talarico super-PAC money spent **$9M against Jasmine Crockett**, and **James Talarico won** the primary (52.4%) — even though Talarico campaigns against super-PAC money. Both primaries are settled: **Paxton (R) vs Talarico (D)** in November. The PACs hide behind friendly, patriotic names that tell you nothing about who funds them — and even when they spend tens of millions, the voters still picked the other candidate.
