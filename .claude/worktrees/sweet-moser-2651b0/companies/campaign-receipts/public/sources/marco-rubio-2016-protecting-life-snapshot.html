Protecting Life at Every Stage

  • Share
  • Tweet
  • Email

Protecting life defines who we want to be as a society. All life is worthy of protection, and all life enjoys God’s love.

Marco believes that Roe v. Wade was not only morally wrong, but it was a poorly decided legal precedent and should be overturned.

Marco has a record of supporting pro-life policies, and will continue to do so in public and private life.

Marco believes that as a nation we must always come down on the side of life. We must speak up for those who cannot speak up for themselves.
