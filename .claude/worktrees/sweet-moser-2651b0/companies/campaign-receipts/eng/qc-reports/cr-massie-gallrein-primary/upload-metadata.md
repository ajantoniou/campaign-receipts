## Description

Thirty-five million dollars in one House primary. The most expensive primary for a single seat in American history. Thomas Massie is out, and the receipts are public.

Kentucky's Fourth district sits across the river from Cincinnati. Massie — a libertarian Republican who voted no on Trump's tax & immigration package, voted no on foreign-aid bills including Israel funding, and forced votes to release the Epstein files — became the most expensive primary loss in U.S. House history. Roughly $16M in outside ads ran against him. Three groups did the heavy lifting: UDP (the AIPAC super-PAC), the Republican Jewish Coalition, and Miriam Adelson's Preserve America PAC. Days before the primary, Massie himself put a number on it on CBS News: "95% of my opponent's money comes from the Israeli lobby." Trump endorsed Ed Gallrein. Gallrein won by about ten thousand votes.
