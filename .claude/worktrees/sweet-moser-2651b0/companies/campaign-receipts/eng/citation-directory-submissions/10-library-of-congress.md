## Why TODO-HUMAN

LoC web-archive nominations are submitted via their "Recommend a Website"
form (https://www.loc.gov/programs/web-archiving/about-this-program/recommend/),
which requires a CAPTCHA + identifying info (nominator name, affiliation).
This is intentionally human-only to keep the corpus high-signal.

## Why worth founder's 5 minutes

A "preserved by Library of Congress" credit is the highest-authority
citation backlink on the open web. Even pending review (which can take
6-12 months), the nomination itself sometimes appears in their public
nomination index.
