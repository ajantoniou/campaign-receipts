## Scene 2 — The waivers (0:15–0:50)

**VO:** "Congress wrote the rule back in nineteen ninety-five: the American embassy belongs in Jerusalem. But the same law gave every president a six-month escape hatch — sign a waiver, push the move, avoid the fight. Clinton, Bush, and Obama all used that hatch, each time warning that moving the building could blow up negotiations and bloodshed in the region. So Tel Aviv stayed the address, and Jerusalem stayed a promise on paper — a promise lobbyists kept paying to keep alive."

The opening date landed on May fourteenth, twenty eighteen — Israel's seventieth birthday on the calendar, whether you read that as symbolism or strategy."
