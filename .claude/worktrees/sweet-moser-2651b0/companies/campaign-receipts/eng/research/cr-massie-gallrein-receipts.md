# Research pack — Massie vs Gallrein (KY-4 R primary, May 19 2026)

## Race summary
- **Race:** Kentucky 4th congressional district, Republican primary, May 19, 2026
- **Incumbent:** Thomas Massie (R-KY-4) — libertarian Republican, in office since 2012
- **Challenger / Winner:** Ed Gallrein — former Navy SEAL, Trump-endorsed
- **Total race spend:** ~$35 million — **most expensive U.S. House primary in history**
- **Outside spending against Massie:** ~$16.4M (Trump-aligned PACs + Israel-lobby PACs)
- **Result (per CNN/Really American transcripts):**
  - Gallrein: ~57,000 votes (~54–55%)
  - Massie: ~47,000 votes (~45%)
  - Margin: ~10,280 votes
- **CNN call:** Earlier than any prior KY primary in this seat (per CNN on-air)

## Why this race got national money

1. **Massie was Trump's chief Republican antagonist in the U.S. House.** Voted against the "big beautiful bill" (Trump tax + immigration package). Voted against foreign-aid packages including Israel/Iron Dome. Forced votes on Epstein-files release.
2. **Trump explicit endorsement of Gallrein.** Posted on Truth Social calling Massie "a totally ineffective loser."
3. **Hegseth (Secretary of War) campaigned in-district** against Massie the weekend before primary day — Massie called this unprecedented in his pre-primary CBS interview.
