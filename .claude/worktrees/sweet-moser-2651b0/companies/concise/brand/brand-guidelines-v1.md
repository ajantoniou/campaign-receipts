## Visual Identity

### Mood Board Selection: "Premium, Clean, Direct"

**Aesthetic Pillars:**
- Minimalist, intentional, high-contrast
- White space as design element
- Strong, modern typography
- Professional but approachable

**Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** Deep navy (#1a2a4d)
- **Secondary:** Pure white (#ffffff)
- **Accent:** Electric lime/teal (#00d9ff or #1dd1a1)
- **Neutral:** Light gray (#f5f5f5), Charcoal (#2c3e50)

**Typography:**
- **Headlines:** Inter Bold or SF Pro Display Bold (modern, geometric sans-serif)
- **Body:** Inter Regular or SF Pro Text (clean, highly legible)
- **Accent/Call-to-action:** Brandon Grotesque or Futura Bold (distinctive, confident)

**Design References:**
- Stripe (minimalist, premium fintech)
- Notion (clean UI, high-contrast type)
- MasterClass (premium positioning, expert-led)

**Feel:** Trustworthy. Expert. No-nonsense. Direct.

## Logo & Wordmark

### "Concise" Wordmark

**Primary Wordmark:**
- Typographic only (no icon lock-up required for Phase 1)
- Typeface: Inter Bold, all-caps, tight letter-spacing
- Color: Navy (#1a2a4d) on white backgrounds; white on navy backgrounds
- Accent underline: Electric lime (#00d9ff), 2-3px thickness, positioned below "E" (partial underline for visual interest)

**Minimum sizing:** 40px width on web, 1" on print

**Variations:**
- Dark on light (primary)
- Light on dark (secondary)
- Monochrome (when color unavailable)
- Single-line alt (for header/footer use): "concise" lowercase, sans underline

## Book Cover System

### Cover Grid Template

**Dimensions:** Standard print (6" × 9" for paperback, 5" × 8" for hardcover front)

**Visual Hierarchy:**
1. **Top band (15%):** "CONCISE" wordmark + accent color bar
2. **Main area (70%):** Book title (large, bold, left-aligned or centered), hero image or typography treatment
3. **Bottom band (15%):** Author name ([Pseudonym]), subtitle/descriptor (optional), spine text

**Color Coding by Category:**
- **MCAT/Education:** Navy primary + accent
- **Politics/Trump:** Navy primary + contrasting secondary color (tbd per CN-009)
- **Advice/Self-help:** Navy primary + warm accent (coral/orange)

**Typography on covers:**
- Titles: Brandon Grotesque Bold or Futura Bold (distinctive, confident)
- Author: Inter Regular or SF Pro (clean, readable)
- "CONCISE" brand mark: Inter Bold, consistent with wordmark

## Brand Voice & Messaging

### Tagline
> "Concise curates and elevates the best educational books — written by experts, designed for direct readers."

### Key Messages
- **Quality:** Every title is expertly written and edited for clarity
- **Direct:** Skip the middleman; support authors and get premium editions
- **Trusted:** 10+ years of reader feedback embedded in every book
- **Diverse:** Topics from MCAT prep to political analysis to practical advice

### Tone
- Professional but conversational
- Authoritative without arrogance
- Direct and clear (no fluff)
- Slightly premium/aspirational

## Notes for Design Team

1. **Pseudonym privacy:** Never reveal founder's real name or credentials in any Concise marketing. Pseudonym is the public face.
2. **Trump book sensitivity:** Per CN-009, keep political messaging platform-agnostic on direct-sale site. (Amazon listings can be more explicit if needed.)
3. **Premium positioning:** Every touchpoint should feel intentional and curated — avoid clip-art, generic stock photography, or overly decorative elements.
4. **Testing:** Once covers are designed, test with 3-5 target readers (pre-med students for MCAT, political readers for Trump book) for clarity + appeal.
