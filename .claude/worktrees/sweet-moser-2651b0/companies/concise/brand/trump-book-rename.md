### Title 4: **UNCOMPROMISED: The Campaign Promises Trump Made Before the Lobbies**
**Sales Hook:** *"Read the uncompromised Trump—his actual 2015-2016 policy positions before foreign money and political deals changed the agenda. A historical document for the record."*

**Why It Works:** "Uncompromised" is the core emotional appeal; directly validates the wedge. Short, memorable, single-word power. Appeals to authenticity seekers and Trump loyalists who feel betrayed by post-presidency pivots. Positions buyer as arbiter of "real" vs. "compromised" versions.

### Title 5: **FOREIGN INFLUENCE: The Promises Trump Made Before It Cost Him**

**Why It Works:** Directly names the wedge in the title. Creates investigative journalism framing. Appeals to readers interested in geopolitical influence, lobbying, and accountability. Positions book as primary source for understanding political pivot analysis.
