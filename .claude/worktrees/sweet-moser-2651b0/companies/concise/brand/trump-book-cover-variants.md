## Variant 1: Standard Neutral Cover

**Concept:** Professional political book cover. Minimal political signaling.

**Design:**
- **Color scheme:** Deep charcoal gray + white + muted accent (olive or slate)
- **Typography:** Bold sans-serif title (e.g., "GRABIT NATION" in 72pt)
- **Imagery:** Abstract geometric shapes OR subtle text layout (quotes snippets)
- **Back cover:** Brief description of content + founder pseudonym + price ($9.99–19.99)
- **Overall feel:** Serious, expert, neutral stance on content

**Visual inspiration:**
- Library of Congress style political book covers
- Neutral news book aesthetics (think: mainstream publisher nonfiction)

**Pros:**
- ✅ Safe for all platforms (Stripe, email, retail partnerships)
- ✅ Appeals to readers seeking objective political analysis
- ✅ Works well for bundling with other titles (less polarizing)
- ✅ Founder can distribute through Amazon, B&N, etc., if desired

**Cons:**
- ❌ Doesn't signal "contrarian" or "bold" positioning
- ❌ Blends into thousands of other political books
- ❌ May underperform vs. bold variant (depends on audience)

**Founder context:** Choose this if you want to reach broad political audience without inflammatory visual signaling.

## Founder Decision Framework

**Ask yourself:**

1. **Am I willing to publicly own this political position?**
   - YES → Variant 2 or 5 (explicit)
   - NO → Variant 1, 3, or 4 (implicit)

2. **Is this book data-driven analysis or political opinion?**
   - Analysis → Variant 4 (credible fact-check framing)
   - Opinion → Variant 2 or 3 (be explicit about perspective)
   - Mixed → Variant 3 (balanced, abstract)

3. **What's my audience?**
   - Existing pseudonym followers → Variant 1 or 3 (familiar but bold)
   - Contrarian political readers → Variant 2 or 4
   - Academic/professional readers → Variant 4 (credible analysis)

4. **Real name + MD credential reveal?**
   - Ready to tie real name to this book → Variant 5
   - Keeping pseudonym → Variants 1-4
