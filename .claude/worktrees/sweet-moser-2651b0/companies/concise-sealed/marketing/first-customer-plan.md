# SEALED — Plan for Customer #1 (pre–full LS checkout)

**Goal:** One paying buyer *or* one qualified lead who converts within 48 hours of checkout going live—without paid ads (portfolio rule at launch).

---

## Positioning (one sentence)

You are selling **verifiable memory**: the 2015–2016 promises *before* dealmaking—not a partisan rant.

---

## Tactics (sequenced)

### 1. Founder-first (Day 0–1)

- Send **5 personal DMs or emails** to people who already trust you and consume politics/history content. Script:

  > “I’m launching a digital archive—Trump’s 2015–16 promises pulled forward next to what happened after. No spin in the PDF—just quotes + timeline. Can I send you the free intro? If it’s useful, you’re my first checkout when payment unlocks.”

- Ask for **one specific introduction** (pod micro-host, Substacks with under ~20k subs, or a moderate politics Discord admin).

### 2. Lead magnet already on site

- **Email capture** delivers intro + first block of quotes—ensure the automated send is reliable (test Resend/Mailchimp path once LS is secondary).

### 3. One channel experiment (pick one, not five)

- **X/Twitter:** Single thread: 6 tweets = 3 quotes from the book + “why archive matters” + link to site + “checkout soon.” No reply-guy debates for 48h.

  **Or**

- **Reddit:** One post in a **history or neutral politics** sub (follow rules; disclose creator). Title focuses on **archive / primary sources**, not outrage.

### 4. “First buyer” incentive (ethical, legal)

- First confirmed purchase after LS approval: **thank-you public shout-out** (optional, only if they agree) **or** private handwritten thank-you—cost ~$0–5.

### 5. Do **not** do before LS approval spends money on ads

- Portfolio rule: organic until traction—keep powder dry.

---

## Metrics (minimum)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Email signups from landing | ≥25 before checkout opens |
| Reply rate on founder outreach | ≥40% |
| Time from LS approval → first sale attempt | &lt;24h (send link to warm list first) |

---

## Checkout-open playbook (the moment LS approves)

1. Email everyone who opted in: **one screen recording** (15s) showing buy → receipt (sandbox or real $1 test).
2. Post **once** where you already have permission (personal profile + one community).
3. Same day: verify **download email + PDF open** on mobile.

## Owner

Brand/Growth executes outreach list; CTO verifies email + LS webhook; CEO owns message tone compliance.
