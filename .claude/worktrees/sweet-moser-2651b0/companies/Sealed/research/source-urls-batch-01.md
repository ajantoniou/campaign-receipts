C-SPAN rally videos exist but are not accessible via automated fetch. This is the hardest-to-source promise in batch 1.

3. **KORUS (#19):** Not mentioned by name in any of the three presidential debates. The source is the archived campaign trade policy page, which is accessible but uses issue-framing language rather than a direct "I will renegotiate KORUS" quote.
