## What Cliros is (and isn't), as of today

Cliros is **informational title research** for GA closing attorneys. It pulls
GSCCCA, federal courts (CourtListener/PACER), county tax parcel, and runs
the index data through a 6-persona AI quality panel before producing:

1. A **title search report** (PDF) summarizing chain, liens, defects, and
   marketability with the underlying source records cited.
2. An **AOL draft** (PDF, Fannie Mae B7-2-06 format) with your firm branding
   and the chain/liens pre-populated.
3. A **plain-English homeowner summary** (PDF) you can hand to your client.
4. A **closing checklist** clustered by lender — one "obtain release" item
   per creditor (not per recorded SD), with a pre-drafted payoff letter
   mailto for each, plus auto-resolved items the system already handled.

**It is NOT a title opinion or title insurance.** Every PDF says so. Every
report disclaims: "informational only — Cliros assembles the research and
the draft; the reviewing attorney provides the legal judgment and signs the
final opinion." The AOL draft is a draft you sign. We don't sign anything
for you.

**Coverage:** GA only. All 159 counties indexed via GSCCCA, but tax-parcel
data quality varies — Fulton, DeKalb, Cobb, Gwinnett are best-tested. Other
counties work but may need a prior-owner-name hint to land cleanly (see
"Optional accuracy hints" on the new-search form).

real time and into the `cliros.beta_feedback` table for our QA panel to
review. Common patterns get rolled into the next sprint. Specific report
errors get re-run for free with the credit returned to your balance.

Your feedback decides what we build next. The founding cohort drives the
roadmap for the next 90 days.

## How to grant the 20 credits

Use the script:

```bash
cd companies/cliros/app
npx tsx scripts/grant_founding_attorney.ts <email> "<Firm Name>"
```

The script:
1. Looks up the user by email in Supabase Auth.
2. Inserts a row into `cliros.report_packages` with `size=20`,
   `amount_cents=0`, `ls_order_id='founding_attorney_<userId>_<timestamp>'`,
   `expires_at=now() + 3 months`.
3. Bumps `users.reports_remaining += 20` and `users.role='founding_attorney'`.
4. Emails the attorney via Resend with welcome copy + login link.

## Tracking

All founding attorneys carry `users.role='founding_attorney'`. Dashboard
query for the cohort:

```sql
SELECT email, name, created_at, reports_remaining,
       (SELECT COUNT(*) FROM cliros.search_reports
        WHERE user_id = u.id AND pipeline_stage IN ('ready','delivered')) AS shipped_reports,
       (SELECT COUNT(*) FROM cliros.beta_feedback
        WHERE user_id = u.id) AS feedback_count
FROM cliros.users u
WHERE role = 'founding_attorney'
ORDER BY created_at DESC;
```

A healthy founding attorney is one with `shipped_reports ≈ feedback_count`
(they're leaving feedback per report). If `shipped_reports >> feedback_count`,
nudge them via email. If `shipped_reports = 0` after 2 weeks, they're stuck
on signup — call them.
