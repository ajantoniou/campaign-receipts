## ✅ What's done and live

### Core engine
- **Parcel anchor** (`src/lib/agents/parcel.ts`) — Fulton County tax-parcel resolver. Resolves any GA address to ParcelID + owner + assessed value before GSCCCA search runs. Kills the "railway companies in chain of title" bug by anchoring all downstream searches on the verified parcel owner instead of street-keyword guessing.
- **GSCCCA agent** (`src/lib/agents/gsccca-http.ts`) — HTTP scraper, 50-year search window, deed-type classifier maps all 40+ GSCCCA codes per official chart. Security Deeds (SD) and Cancellations (CANC) route to liens section, not chain of title.
- **CourtListener integration** (`src/lib/agents/courtlistener.ts`) — free federal records search (PACER mirror). Strict debtor-name match prevents cross-contamination (Marcus Labat false positive killed).
- **Atlanta permits** (`src/lib/agents/permits.ts`) — 38,107 permits cached from City of Atlanta ArcGIS CSV, refreshed daily at 9 AM UTC via cron. Flags open-permit mechanics-lien risk as a defect.
- **AI defamation guardrail** (`sanitizeDefects()` in `search-orchestrator.ts`) — strips fraud allegations against real named persons. Compliance/VC/design personas all flagged this as ship-blocking.

### Production pipeline
- **6-persona expert panel + orchestrator** (`src/lib/pipeline/panel.ts`) — every report runs through 6 AI domain experts (closing attorney, title-co owner, compliance counsel, design lead, growth lead, legal-tech VC) in parallel via `claude-opus-4-7`. Orchestrator synthesizes into `ship`/`fix`/`kill`. ~$1.50 per report. Records each persona's verdict + blocking issues in `cliros.report_qa_reviews`.
- **PDF generators** (`src/lib/pipeline/pdf.ts`, `homeowner-template.ts`) — three deliverables per report:
  1. Title Search Report (firm-agnostic legal record)
  2. Attorney Opinion Letter (Fannie Mae B7-2-06 compliant, branded with firm E&O/IOLTA/underwriter fields)
  3. Plain-English Homeowner Summary (branded with firm logo + GA Bar 7.1–7.5 advertising footer)
- **Pipeline state machine** in `cliros.search_reports.pipeline_stage`: `queued → searching → permits → panel_review → drafting → ready → delivered`. `blocked` is terminal if panel kills the report.
- **`scripts/run_pipeline_tick.ts`** — Render cron every 1 min. Atomic claim via Postgres RPC `cliros_claim_next_pipeline_report` (SELECT FOR UPDATE SKIP LOCKED). Advances one stage per tick.
