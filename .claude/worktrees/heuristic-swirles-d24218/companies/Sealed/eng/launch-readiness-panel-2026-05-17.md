You do not have a 9:16 verdict-card pack the populist-skeptic creator tier can overlay on their own content. Press is one channel. Creator pickup is the channel that compounds. Launching press without the creator asset means if Tangle bites, the spike comes to a storefront with no second-order share mechanic. Flip to yes when 10 verdict cards exist, watermarked, downloadable from `/share`.

**Q2. Order.** Day 1: ship the 9:16 verdict-card pack. Ten cards, one per top-engagement promise. Watermark `sealed2016.com` bottom-right, small enough nobody crops it. Day 2: founder posts ONE card on X with the launch tweet — not the storefront link, the *card*. People share images, not URLs. Day 3: press pitches go. Proof print is irrelevant to this lane — creators don't care about paperback weight. Soft-launch with the card pack and X first; press follows the social signal.

Failure mode.** Founder fires the launch tweet, gets 12 likes from existing followers, no creator picks it up because there's nothing to pick up, the tweet dies in 6 hours, founder reads the silence as "doesn't work," pulls the press pitches in shame. The actual problem was no shareable asset, not no demand.

## Synthesis

**Q1 consensus: CONDITIONAL — do not fire press this week.** Three of five panelists say conditional, two say hold, zero say ready. The conditions stack: editor handles unverified (journalist), no creator asset (viral), pitch framing buries the news (bestseller), mobile/cover unverified (design), payment rail unverified end-to-end (CRO).

**Highest-priority blocker:** The CRO consultant's gate is the only one that is genuinely *unrecoverable* if it fails under load. Editor names and pitch framing can be fixed mid-week. A blown payment rail under a Pirate Wires spike cannot. **Day-0 move: one real non-founder $15 order end-to-end. No press fires until that buyer confirms receipt.**

**Recommended Day-1:** Verify editor handles + `press@` inbox + run the friend test order. Day-2: rewrite pitch subject lines to lead with the 2024 deletion. Day-3: ship 9:16 verdict-card pack (parallelized to viral lane). Day-4: Tangle pitch fires solo. Day-5+: PW + FP if Tangle is silent by 48h; Bari held until personal angle filled and one bite logged.

**Panel tweet pick:** Bestseller's draft wins on news-lede discipline. Final:
> The 2024 Trump campaign quietly deleted its official platform page. We preserved every word. Then we built a scorecard for what he promised in 2016 and what he actually did. 46 kept. 51 broken. 40 partial. Read it: sealed2016.com

**Minority view worth surfacing:** Viral guru's "no creator asset = press spike has no second-order compounding." If founder has to choose between firing press this week without the card pack vs. delaying one week to ship the pack first, the answer is delay. Press is a one-shot; creator pickup is the flywheel.
