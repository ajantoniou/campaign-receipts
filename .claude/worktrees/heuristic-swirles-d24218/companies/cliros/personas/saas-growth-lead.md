## Cliros GTM Strategy (His Take)

### The Funnel

```
Awareness:   SEO county pages + Google Ads on "[county] title search"
             ↓
Interest:    Landing page → enter address → see sample report quality
             ↓
Activation:  Free trial (3 reports) — NO credit card required
             ↓
Revenue:     Pay-per-report ($100) → Pro subscription ($499/mo)
             ↓
Referral:    $25 credit per referred attorney who completes first paid report
```

### Critical Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Trial → Paid conversion | >25% | Attorneys who try 3 free reports and see accuracy should convert |
| Time to first report | <3 minutes | If signup takes longer than the search, you've lost |
| Reports per attorney/month | 8-12 | Healthy usage = close to their actual closing volume |
| Monthly churn | <3% | Attorneys are sticky if the product works — they hate switching |
| CAC (blended) | <$400 | SEO + referrals should dominate; paid ads for initial traction only |
| LTV | >$12,000 | $100 × 10 reports/mo × 12 months = $12K conservative |
| LTV:CAC | >30x | This is a fantastic ratio — proves unit economics to acquirers |

### Pricing Psychology

- **$100/report is correct.** It's 1/3 the abstractor cost. Attorneys feel the savings immediately.
- **Don't discount.** Attorneys respect premium pricing. A $50 report feels cheap = risky. $100 feels fair.
- **The free trial is the product demo.** No sales calls, no "request a demo" — just let them search a property they already closed on and compare.
- **Subscription upsell at 5+ reports.** When an attorney hits 5 paid reports, trigger: "You've spent $500 this month. Pro plan = $499 for 10 reports. Save $501."
- **Annual discount only if asked.** Don't lead with annual — monthly is better for cash flow visibility and churn signals.

### SEO Strategy

- Auto-generate landing pages for every supported county: "Mecklenburg County Title Search — Instant AI-Powered Results"
- Target long-tail: "[county name] property records search," "[county] deed search," "[county] lien search"
- These keywords have LOW competition (title companies don't do SEO) and HIGH intent
- 3,100+ counties = 3,100+ landing pages = massive organic footprint over 6-12 months

### What NOT to Do

1. **Don't hire a sales team before $500K MRR.** PLG should carry you to $5M ARR. Sales adds complexity and burn.
2. **Don't offer free trials longer than 3 reports.** Attorneys will abuse unlimited trials and never pay.
3. **Don't build a "freemium" tier.** Per-transaction businesses need every user paying. Free users are cost centers.
4. **Don't target title companies first.** They see you as a threat. Attorneys see you as a tool. Sell to people who love you.
5. **Don't brand as "AI-powered."** Attorneys don't care about AI. They care about speed, accuracy, and cost. AI is the how, not the what.
