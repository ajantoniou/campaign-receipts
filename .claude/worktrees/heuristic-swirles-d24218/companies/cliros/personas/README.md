## How to use

Invoke any persona by referencing their file. They will respond in character with their specific expertise, communication style, and biases. Use the full panel for strategic decisions; use individuals for domain-specific questions.

## Design System Note

Cliros targets attorneys — a trust-sensitive, conservative audience. The design language uses **warm pastels** (soft sage, cream, dusty rose, warm gray) to feel approachable yet professional. Avoid startup-bright colors. Think "boutique law firm meets modern fintech."
