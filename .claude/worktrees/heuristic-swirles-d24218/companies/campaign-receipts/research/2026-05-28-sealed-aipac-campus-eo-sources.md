## Receipt 2 — Executive Order 13899 (Dec 11, 2019)

- **Claim:** On December 11, 2019, EO 13899 directed federal agencies to *consider* the IHRA working definition of antisemitism (and its contemporary examples) when enforcing Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964.

- **Operative text (Tier 1, verbatim from the order, Sec. 2(a)):**

- **First-Amendment safeguard (Tier 1, verbatim, Sec. 2(b)):**
  > "In considering the materials described in subsections (a)(i) and (a)(ii) of this section, agencies shall not diminish or infringe upon any right protected under Federal law or under the First Amendment… the inquiry… will require a detailed analysis of the allegations."
- **Tier:** 1 (PRIMARY, verbatim).
- **Discrepancies / nuance for Stage 7 (IMPORTANT for the "earn BROKEN" lock):**
  - The order says agencies **"shall consider"** the definition — it does **not** make the definition itself a binding rule, and it expressly preserves First-Amendment rights and requires a "detailed analysis."
