## Notes for the appeal

Line-by-line map of which sentence carries which legitimacy signal, so the
founder can cite specifics in the appeal text.

- **"Campaign Receipts is a political-media company."** — Opening sentence
  states the category plainly. First thing a reviewer or classifier reads:
  this is journalism/media, not a scheme.
- **"We tie donor money to the public record: ... votes ... bills ...
  promises ... races"** — States the editorial method and the four things
  every piece covers (congressional votes, sponsored bills, promises
  kept/broken, races won/lost). Concrete subject matter, not a pitch.
- **"Every fact comes from US-government .gov sources. ... FEC.gov ...
  Congress.gov. Public-record data, anyone can check it."** — The core
  trust signal. Independently verifiable government data. This is the
  single strongest line against a "scam" classification.
- **"No spin, no fluff, just the documented record. Receipts, not vibes."**
  — Editorial standard + established tagline. No financial-advice tone, no
  promise of returns.
- **"We follow the money regardless of party."** — Honest framing of
  even-handedness without a false non-partisanship claim.
- **"Editorial lead: Alex Antoniou."** — Named, accountable human editor.
- **"Every verdict is reviewed by a three-person panel ... public audit
  trail."** — Editorial accountability process: cross-spectrum review
  (neutral, conservative, progressive) with public review notes. Shows a
  real editorial workflow, not automated content farming.
- **"Read the full record, free, at campaignreceipts.com. Weekly newsletter
  at campaignreceipts.com/weekly."** — Free public website (nothing
  gated/sold) + newsletter. "Free" rebuts any monetization-scam read.
- **"Narration uses a synthesized voice (ElevenLabs Jessica). Research,
  scripts, and commentary are by Campaign Receipts."** — Honest disclosure
  of method. Transparency about AI narration strengthens the legitimacy
  case rather than hiding it. (Verbatim from the disclosure language already
  applied across all 35 uploads per channel-audit-2026-05-28.md.)
