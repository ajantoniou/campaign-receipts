## Notes on judgment calls

- **The dollar-figure-in-caps titles ($35 MILLION / $8 MILLION leading) are KEPT** where the rest of the title is mixed-case and sober (7GpuZ0cfK1Y, QP6rXu_bFDk). A leading dollar amount in caps is the established CR thumbnail/number convention, not shouting, and the persona doc names "$35 MILLION Beat Thomas Massie" as the canonical pattern. Where a full word was *also* shouted ("ONE"), I de-shouted just that word and kept the title (−Zbufm6Un_s, dJBhyU6MILU).
- **Death/kill language:** kept only where literal and sourced, stated soberly. "60 Killed at the Gaza Border" replaces the bare "60 DEAD" tragedy-bait phrasing. "Killed Obamacare repeal" became "Sank Obamacare repeal" — "sank" is the literal vote outcome (49-51) and removes a kill-word the classifier would flag with no loss of fact.
- **SEALED Kept:/Broken: prefixes retained** as a legit mixed-case editorial label; the `| SEALED` tag stays for series identity.
