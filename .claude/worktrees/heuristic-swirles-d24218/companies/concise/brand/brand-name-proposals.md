# Concise — Brand Name Proposals

**Owner:** Brand/Design

**Due:** Saturday 2 PM (DELIVERED)

**Context:** Decide whether to continue existing Amazon pseudonym brand OR launch fresh "Concise" direct-sale brand OR hybrid.

**Founder decision:** Approves direction for Week 1 launch.

---

## Context: Current Brand State

- **Existing brand:** Pseudonym on Amazon (10 years, $200/mo revenue)
- **Direct-sale launch:** Same pseudonym OR new direct-sale brand?
- **Consideration:** Amazon requires separation if using different brand; founder can use same pseudonym both places OR create "Concise" as parent brand with pseudonym as author name

---

## Option 1: Continue Pseudonym (Same Brand Everywhere)

**Concept:** Reuse existing pseudonym brand across direct-sale channel. No change, no confusion.

**Pros:**
- ✅ Existing audience recognition (10 years, $200/mo signal)
- ✅ No brand education needed
- ✅ Simple email/social coordination
- ✅ Amazon listings feed into existing brand awareness

**Cons:**
- ❌ Pseudonym may not evoke "premium direct-sale" positioning
- ❌ Limited ability to create premium sub-brands (e.g., "Concise MCAT Elite")
- ❌ Direct-sale brand can't differentiate visually from Amazon listings without confusion

**Best for:** Cost minimization, immediate launch, founder who values simplicity.

**Recommendation:** Viable if founder wants fastest path to launch.

---

## Option 2: "Concise" Parent Brand + Pseudonym Author

**Concept:** "Concise" is the master brand. Pseudonym is the author. Clear visual + messaging separation between Amazon (pseudonym-first) and direct-sale (Concise-first).

**Example:**
- Direct-sale landing page: "**Concise** — premium book editions by [Pseudonym]"
- Book cover: "**Concise** / MCAT Essentials / by [Pseudonym]"
- Amazon: "[Pseudonym] / MCAT Essentials" (existing)

**Pros:**
- ✅ "Concise" signals punchy, direct, premium
- ✅ Clear separation from Amazon (different look, different feel)
- ✅ Allows sub-brands: "Concise MCAT" vs. "Concise Politics" vs. "Concise Advice"
- ✅ Direct-sale can charge premium pricing ("Concise" implies quality)
- ✅ Founder can later reveal real name on "Concise Medical" without affecting Amazon pseudonym brand
- ✅ Flexible for Phase 2 pivots (AI coach under "Concise Labs"?)

**Cons:**
- ❌ Requires brand education (who is Concise? why?)
- ❌ Two logos to manage
- ❌ Slight complexity for founder: explain relationship between pseudonym + Concise

**Best for:** Premium positioning, multi-channel strategy, founder who wants optionality.

**Recommendation:** **STRONGEST OPTION** — balances freshness, premium signal, and founder privacy.

---

## Option 3: Complete Rebranding (Leave Amazon Behind, New Brand)

**Concept:** Abandon existing pseudonym brand entirely. Launch fresh brand for direct-sale.

**Example candidates:**
- "Concise" (punchy, memorable, brand-ready)
- "Clarity" (expert education, clear explanations)
- "Essentials" (get to the point, no fluff)
- "[Founder's real name] Books" (highest trust, most personal)

**Pros:**
- ✅ Clean slate, no legacy baggage
- ✅ Full creative control (visuals, voice, messaging)
- ✅ Can position as founder's "real work" (vs. Amazon pseudonym as side hustle)
- ✅ Real name reveal becomes story, not secret ("I wrote under pseudonym for 10 years, now launching direct")

**Cons:**
- ❌ Lose $200/mo Amazon revenue signal (Amazon books may not cross-sell to direct)
- ❌ Requires education + marketing spend (build awareness from scratch)
- ❌ Ignores 10 years of audience (pre-med students on Amazon don't know about "Concise")

**Best for:** Founder who wants complete control, has budget for brand awareness, views Amazon books as legacy.

**Recommendation:** Risky. Leaves existing revenue + audience on table.

## Comparative Matrix

| Factor | Option 1 (Pseudonym) | Option 2 (Concise + Pseudonym) | Option 3 (New Brand) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Launch speed** | Fastest (1 week) | Fast (1 week + brand design) | Slow (2 weeks, brand education) |
| **Amazon integration** | Strong (same brand) | Good (related brands) | Weak (separate brands) |
| **Premium positioning** | Weak | Strong | Varies |
| **Flexibility** | Low | High | High |
| **Founder complexity** | Low | Medium | Medium |
| **Risk** | Low | Low | Medium (audience loss) |

## Recommendation for Founder

**STRONGLY RECOMMEND: Option 2 ("Concise" + Pseudonym)**

**Rationale:**
1. Retains $200/mo Amazon revenue + audience signal
2. Creates premium direct-sale brand with "Concise" positioning
3. Allows founder to later reveal real name (MD credential) on premium MCAT books without affecting pseudonym brand
4. Enables Phase 2 sub-brands ("Concise Medical," "Concise Politics," "Concise Advice")
5. Clear visual differentiation: Amazon looks like "traditional book," direct-sale looks "premium, curated"

**Messaging framework:**
> "**Concise** curates and elevates the best educational books — written by experts, designed for direct readers. Each title cuts through the noise and delivers exactly what you need."
