## Overview

Per SESSION_DECISIONS.md (2026-05-02 23:00 ET), founder is leaning toward **"candidate before influence-capture" wedge** as the brand positioning for the Trump book.

Three seed titles ranked by founder preference:
1. "Trump Before AIPAC Money"
2. "Trump's America First Promises: Prior to Iran War"
3. "Trump's America First Promises: Prior to Foreign Lobby Influence"

**This document presents 5 cover variants** for the top-ranked title, all designed to support the "pro-original-Trump, anti-influence-capture" wedge positioning.

---

## Design Constraints (MANDATORY)

These cover designs **must not**:
- ❌ Include photographs or images of Trump
- ❌ Include flag imagery (US flag, AIPAC flag, or other)
- ❌ Include money imagery (despite "money" in title)
- ❌ Include inflammatory symbols or rhetoric
- ❌ Make explicit foreign policy statements through imagery
- ❌ Include faces or real people

**Why:** Political sensitivity, retail platform compliance, ability to sell on Amazon/direct without triggering policy flags

**These covers must**:
- ✅ Use abstract/geometric treatment
- ✅ Use typography as the primary visual element
- ✅ Signal "analytical, contrarian, serious" tone
- ✅ Support Concise brand identity (navy + gold accent)
- ✅ Be seller-neutral (no obvious political team colors)

### Specifications

**Design approach:** Typography-dominant, minimal decoration

- Light gray background (#f5f5f5)
- No photographs or visual imagery

- Minimum whitespace to maximize title impact

**Rationale:**

- Strongest for retail compliance (no visual controversy)
- Premium positioning through clean design
- Stands out on digital storefronts (text-heavy covers uncommon)

### Specifications

**Design approach:** Typographic layering (text as visual element)

**Color palette:**
- Navy (#1a2a4d) — primary text
- White (#ffffff) — background, shadow/layering effects
- Gold (#d4af37) — optional accent (small elements only)
- Subtle gray (#e0e0e0) — shadow/layering depth

**Typography:**
- Title: Futura Bold or Brandon Grotesque Bold, very large (2"+)
- Layering technique: Same text duplicated, offset by 8-12px, creating 3D depth effect
- Subtitle: Inter Regular, 11pt

- Text layering (double-exposure or shadow effect)
- No photographs or decorative imagery
- Typography creates the visual interest entirely
- Sophisticated, modern, premium feel

**Design principle:**
- **Layering suggests complexity and depth**
- **Multiple readings of same text** suggest multiple perspectives
- **Sophisticated design appeals to educated audience** (target: policy-aware readers)

**Rationale:**
- **Highly distinctive** — unusual typographic treatment
- **Sophisticated feel** — appeals to analytical audience
- **Metaphorically rich** — layering suggests depth and multiple interpretations
- Risk: May be hard to read at small thumbnail size

### Approval Level
**PROBABLY SAFE** (no controversial imagery, but depends on execution clarity)
