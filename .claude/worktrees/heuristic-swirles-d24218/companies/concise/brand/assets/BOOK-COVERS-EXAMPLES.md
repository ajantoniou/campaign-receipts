## Example 1: MCAT Essentials

### Book Details
- **Title:** MCAT Essentials
- **Subtitle:** Proven Strategies for Test Day Success
- **Author:** [Pseudonym Author Name]
- **Category:** Education / Medical
- **Format:** Paperback 6" × 9"

### Visual Design Specification

#### Top Band (15% height)
- **Background color:** Navy (#1a2a4d) with electric cyan accent line
- **Content:**
  - "CONCISE" wordmark (Inter Bold, navy, 0.5" height)
  - Cyan underline beneath wordmark (3px, full width)
  - Optional: small medical icon (stethoscope or molecules, right side, 0.25" × 0.25")

#### Main Content Area (70% height)
- **Layout:** Centered typography with hero image
- **Title:** "MCAT ESSENTIALS"
  - Font: Brandon Grotesque Bold or Futura Bold
  - Size: 2" height
  - Color: Navy (#1a2a4d)
  - Position: Centered horizontally, upper-middle vertically
  - Spacing below: 0.25"
- **Subtitle:** "Proven Strategies for Test Day Success"
  - Font: Inter Regular
  - Size: 14pt
  - Color: Navy
  - Position: Centered below title
  - Max width: 4.5" (avoid edges)
- **Hero Image:** Medical/science themed
  - Placement: Lower 50% of main area, centered
  - Suggested imagery:
    - Stethoscope on blue gradient background
    - Brain scan cross-section
    - Medical textbook pages
    - Abstract medical molecules/atoms
  - Image mask: Optional subtle rounded corners (6px)
  - Overlay: Optional 5% dark overlay for readability if needed

#### Bottom Band (15% height)
- **Background:** Light gray (#f5f5f5)
- **Author line:** "by [Pseudonym Author Name]"
  - Font: Inter Regular
  - Size: 12pt
  - Color: Navy
  - Position: Left-aligned, 0.5" from left, 0.5" from bottom
- **Category label (optional):** "MCAT PREP"
  - Font: Inter Light
  - Size: 10pt
  - Color: Navy
  - Position: Right-aligned, 0.5" from right, 0.5" from bottom

### Color Palette
- Primary Navy: #1a2a4d
- Accent Cyan: #00d9ff
- Background: White
- Bottom band: Light gray #f5f5f5
- Text: Navy
- Accent underline: Cyan

### Design Rationale
- Clean, trustworthy, education-forward aesthetic
- Medical/scientific imagery reinforces credibility
- Cyan accent signals modernity + premium positioning
- Navy text ensures high readability at thumbnail size

### Figma Notes
- Use master components: `TopBand-MCAT`, `MainContent-Centered`, `BottomBand`
- Image placeholder: 2400px × 1800px (high-res for print)
- Export at 300 DPI for print, 72 DPI for web

### Print Specifications
- Format: Paperback 6" × 9"
- Bleed: 0.125" on all sides
- Color space: CMYK
- Safe margin: 0.25" from edges
- Font embedding: Required (Brandon Grotesque or substitute)

## Example 2: Trump Before AIPAC Money

### Book Details
- **Title:** Trump Before AIPAC Money
- **Subtitle:** A Candidate's First Year and the Capture That Followed
- **Author:** [Pseudonym Author Name]
- **Category:** Politics / Analysis
- **Format:** Paperback 6" × 9"
- **Sensitivity:** Per CN-009, avoid inflammatory imagery. Use abstract/typographic treatment.

### Visual Design Specification

#### Top Band (15% height)
- **Background color:** Navy (#1a2a4d) with gold accent line
- **Content:**
  - "CONCISE" wordmark (Inter Bold, navy, 0.5" height)
  - Gold underline beneath wordmark (3px, full width)
  - Optional: abstract geometric pattern (right side, subtle, 0.25" × 0.25")

#### Main Content Area (70% height)
- **Layout:** Bold typography with abstract background
- **Title line 1:** "TRUMP BEFORE"
  - Font: Brandon Grotesque Bold or Futura Bold
  - Size: 1.8" height
  - Color: Navy (#1a2a4d) or white (contrast-dependent)
  - Position: Centered horizontally, upper third
  - Spacing below: 0.1"
- **Title line 2:** "AIPAC MONEY"
  - Same font/size as line 1
  - Color: Gold (#d4af37) or white
  - Position: Immediately below title line 1
  - Bold impact through contrasting colors or weight
- **Subtitle:** "A Candidate's First Year and the Capture That Followed"
  - Font: Inter Regular
  - Size: 12pt
  - Color: White or navy (contrast with background)
  - Position: Centered below titles, 0.5" spacing
  - Max width: 4.5"
- **Background image/pattern:** Abstract geometric treatment
  - NO photographs or inflammatory imagery
  - Suggested approach:
    - Bold geometric shapes (circles, triangles, lines)
    - Color blocking (navy + gold + white)
    - Gradient overlay (navy to lighter shade)
    - Minimalist abstract pattern (waves, grids, etc.)
  - Alternative: Solid colored background with subtle texture
  - Effect: Conveys "bold, contrarian, analytical" without visual controversy

#### Bottom Band (15% height)
- **Background:** Light gray (#f5f5f5)
- **Author line:** "by [Pseudonym Author Name]"
  - Font: Inter Regular
  - Size: 12pt
  - Color: Navy
  - Position: Left-aligned, 0.5" from left, 0.5" from bottom
- **Category label:** "POLITICAL ANALYSIS"
  - Font: Inter Light
  - Size: 10pt
  - Color: Navy
  - Position: Right-aligned, 0.5" from right, 0.5" from bottom

### Color Palette
- Primary Navy: #1a2a4d
- Accent Gold: #d4af37
- Background: Navy or white (with contrasting text)
- Bottom band: Light gray #f5f5f5
- Secondary accent: Warm orange #ff6b35 (optional for geometric shapes)
- Text: Navy or white (high contrast)

### Design Rationale
- Bold typography dominates (positions title as the visual focus)
- Abstract background avoids sensitivity/controversy while signaling "serious political analysis"
- Gold accent conveys "premium, influential, curated expertise"
- No faces, no inflammatory imagery, no flag symbolism
- Geometric treatment = modern, analytical, forward-thinking

### Design Constraints (CRITICAL)
- ❌ NO photographs of Trump or political figures
- ❌ NO flag imagery (US, AIPAC, or other)
- ❌ NO money imagery (bills, coins, etc. — despite "money" in title)
- ❌ NO inflammatory visual metaphors
- ✅ SAFE: Abstract typography, geometric patterns, gradient overlays, solid colors

### Figma Notes
- Use master components: `TopBand-Politics`, `MainContent-LeftAligned`, `BottomBand`
- Background: Solid color or designer-created abstract pattern
- Font: Brandon Grotesque Bold with tight letter spacing
- Consider: Color-blocking with gold on navy, or white title on navy background

### Print Specifications
- Format: Paperback 6" × 9"
- Bleed: 0.125" on all sides
- Color space: CMYK (gold requires accurate Pantone reference: 871 C)
- Safe margin: 0.25" from edges
- Fonts: Embedded or outlined (no subset)

  - Size: 10pt

- Primary Navy: #1a2a4d
- Accent Coral: #ff6b6b

## Export Workflow

### For Each Example Cover:

1. **Web PNG (1000×1500px)**
   ```
   Format: PNG
   Resolution: 72 DPI (web)
   Width: 1000px
   Height: 1500px
   Background: Transparent (if bleed removed)
   Color space: sRGB
   ```

2. **Print PDF (6"×9" with bleeds)**
   ```
   Format: PDF
   Resolution: 300 DPI
   Trim size: 6" × 9"
   Bleed: 0.125" on all sides
   Color space: CMYK
   Fonts: Embedded (required for print)
   ```

3. **Social Media PNG (1000×1000px square)**
   ```
   Format: PNG
   Resolution: 72 DPI
   Square crop: 1000×1000px (centered on title)
   Color space: sRGB
   ```
