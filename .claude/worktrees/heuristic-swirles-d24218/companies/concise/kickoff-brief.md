## Welcome

You are joining the founding team of **Concise Turnaround**, a book
business pivot.

The founder owns 20+ books in their CONCISE Google Drive folder, sold
under pseudonym on Amazon for 10 years generating ~$200/mo. The
founder is also a board-certified physician — credential that's
underleveraged on Amazon.

**Your mission:** turn this from a $200/mo passive Amazon hustle into
a $400-1000/mo direct-sale business by repackaging existing IP,
optimizing distribution, and pivoting strategy based on data.

**Critical:** founder ALREADY wrote the books. You don't write content.
You repackage, design, distribute, and learn what sells.

## Mission

Ship direct-sale PDF distribution for 3 books in week 1. First sale
by week 3. Reach $400-1,000/mo MRR by day 90.

## Hard non-negotiables

1. **$250 budget cap.**
2. **Don't kill existing Amazon revenue.** $200/mo continues. Track
   separately.
3. **Founder doesn't write new content** during Phase 1. Repackage
   only.
4. **Founder approves Trump book cover direction** — political
   statement is theirs to own, not agents'.
5. **Founder owns pseudonym vs real-name decision.** Don't reveal
   credentials without explicit founder approval.
6. **No HIPAA, no health claims, no telehealth.** Even on MCAT
   books — they're educational/exam-prep, not medical advice.

## Week 1 mission

By end of weekend:
- Brand direction approved
- Domain registered
- Render web service stub deployed
- 3 candidate books identified for direct-sale launch
- Cover variants drafted (especially for Trump book)
- Founder approves cover direction

By end of week 1:
- Landing pages live for 3 books
- Stripe Payment Links configured
- PDF delivery automated
- First Reddit + TikTok organic engagement

By end of week 4:
- 5+ books direct-sale live
- Email nurture sequence active
- Bundle pricing working (MCAT bundle especially)
- First sales (target 5-15 sales by EOW 4)

By day 90:
- $400-1,000/mo total revenue ($200 Amazon + $200-800 direct)

## Decision authority

- **CEO:** strategy, pivot decisions, $20-50 spend
- **CTO:** infrastructure, code
- **Brand/Design:** visual direction (founder approves controversial
  covers)
- **Founder approves:**
  - Brand name + domain
  - Trump book cover direction
  - Pseudonym vs real-name strategy
  - Pivot decisions (AI coach? new advice books?)
  - Anything reframing Concise's brand publicly

## Critical risk areas

1. **Existing Amazon revenue erosion.** Mitigation: track Amazon vs
   direct separately; don't pull books from Amazon.
2. **Controversial cover backlash.** Mitigation: founder approves
   explicitly knowing trade-offs.
3. **Pseudonym + credential tension.** Mitigation: founder decides.
4. **Books market truly dying.** Mitigation: pivot evaluation monthly;
   AI coach or new advice books are real Phase 3 paths.

## Failure modes

Specific to Concise:
- "Let's launch all 20 books at once" — too much surface, focus on
  3 quality launches first
- Engineering perfectionism on landing pages — they're simple, ship
- "Let's pivot to AI coach in week 1" — no, that's Phase 3 after data
- Spammy Reddit promotion — banned in target subs, build trust first

## Final note

This is the **lowest-risk active company** because IP and revenue
already exist. Don't overthink it. Pull books from Drive, design
covers, ship landing pages, drive traffic, learn.

3 books direct-sale by EOW 1. First sale by EOW 3. $400+ MRR by day 90.
