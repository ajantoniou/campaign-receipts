# CON-78 — Quote verification batch (appendix note)

This appendix note records the 10 promises that will anchor the SEALED appendix and links each quote to a contemporaneous primary source. Use this as the provenance layer for the “promise → reality” tracking spreadsheet and the manuscript footer.

1. **Border wall (June 16, 2015).**  
   “It is way past time to build a massive wall to secure our southern border – and nobody can build a bigger and better wall than Donald Trump.”  
   Source: June 16 2015 announcement speech transcript. [p2016.org/trump061615sp.html](http://www.p2016.org/trump/trump061615sp.html)  
   Verification: The transcript (lines ~183‑189) captures the quote verbatim, immediately after Trump’s declaration that “It is time to take our country in a bold new direction.” The adjacent sentence—“A country without borders is, quite simply, not a country”—confirms the surrounding context.

2. **Obamacare repeal (June 16, 2015).**  
   “ObamaCare must be repealed and replaced with something far superior and at far less cost.”  
   Source: same transcript above.  
   Verification: Lines ~201‑208 of the same speech show Trump pledging to protect seniors and then saying “ObamaCare must be repealed and replaced with something far superior and at far less cost,” matching the quoted wording.

3. **Trade deals & job relocation (June 16, 2015).**  
   “It is time to stop sending jobs overseas through bad foreign trade deals. We will renegotiate our trade deals with the toughest negotiators our country has…”  
   Source: same transcript above.  
   Verification: The transcript lines ~212‑215 continue the paragraph with that exact promise, including “the ones who have actually read ‘The Art of the Deal’ and know how to make great deals for our country,” so the context and wording align.

4. **Senior safety net (June 16, 2015).**  
   “The government must honor its obligations to our seniors. We must protect Social Security, Medicare and Medicaid, without cuts.”  
   Source: same transcript above.  
   Verification: The lines just before the Obamacare pledge (≈201‑204) list the same three programs and note that there “will no longer be any waste, fraud and abuse on my watch,” so this wording matches the file’s quoted sentence.

5. **Defeating ISIS (June 16, 2015).**  
   “It is time to defeat ISIS. With a proper plan, it can be done quickly and effectively.”  
   Source: same transcript above.  
   Verification: Lines ~232‑239 document Trump saying “It is time to defeat ISIS. With a proper plan, it can be done quickly and effectively,” immediately after he promises to stand by Israel and stop Iran from developing nuclear weapons.

6. **“Jobs president” promise (June 16, 2015).**  
   “I will be the greatest jobs president that God ever created. I’ll bring back our jobs and I’ll bring back our money.”  
   Source: Newsmax coverage of the announcement speech. [newsmax.com/streettalk/donald-trump-president-jobs-money/650750](https://cloudflarepoc.newsmax.com/finance/streettalk/donald-trump-president-jobs-money/2015/06/16/id/650750/)  
   Verification: Newsmax’s article verbatim quotes this sentence (see the paragraph that begins “I will be the greatest jobs president…”). It captures the same punctuation and the two clauses “I’ll bring back our jobs and I’ll bring back our money.”

7. **NAFTA (September 2015).**  
   “NAFTA is ‘a disaster,’ and I’ll renegotiate it if I’m elected president—fair trade, not free trade.”  
   Source: 60 Minutes interview coverage. [cbc.ca/news/business/nafta-a-disaster-and-i-ll-renegotiate-says-donald-trump-1.3244280](http://cbc.ca/news/business/nafta-a-disaster-and-i-ll-renegotiate-says-donald-trump-1.3244280)  
   Verification: The CBC article (text captured via https://r.jina.ai/…) quotes Trump saying NAFTA is “a disaster” and that he’ll renegotiate it if elected. The same text also includes “We need fair trade. Not free trade. It’s got to be fair,” matching the appended clause.

8. **“Something terrific” healthcare (July 29, 2015).**  
   “Repeal and replace with something terrific.”  
   Source: Dana Bash interview transcript. [msnbc.com/rachel-maddow-show/repeal-and-replace-something-terrific-msna650576](https://www.msnbc.com/rachel-maddow-show/repeal-and-replace-something-terrific-msna650576)  
   Verification: MSNBC’s story reproduces the Bash/Trump exchange, including the > block that reads “BASH: ... TRUMP: Repeal and replace with something terrific.” That transcript snippet confirms the exact phrasing of the quote.

9. **Energy independence (May 26, 2016).**  
   “Under my presidency, we’ll accomplish a complete American energy independence. Every dollar of energy we don’t explore here is a dollar of energy that makes somebody else rich.”  
   Source: North Dakota energy speech. [cnn.com/2016/05/26/politics/donald-trump-energy-independence-north-dakota](https://www.cnn.com/2016/05/26/politics/donald-trump-energy-independence-north-dakota/?iid=EL)  
   Verification: CNN’s account of the Williston Basin Petroleum Conference includes the same wording—“Under my presidency...complete American energy independence” followed by the “dollar of energy...makes somebody else rich” clause—so the quote is verbatim.

10. **Tax overhaul (September 28, 2015).**  
   “It will provide major tax relief for middle income and for most other Americans. There will be a major tax reduction. It’ll simplify the tax code…”  
   Source: Trump tax plan launch press conference. [cnn.com/2015/09/28/politics/donald-trump-tax-plan](https://www.cnn.com/2015/09/28/politics/donald-trump-tax-plan)  
   Verification: CNN’s coverage of the September 28 announcement quotes Trump saying those exact sentences at Trump Tower, then notes he will pair them with a “drastic” reduction and simplification of the code, so the excerpt matches the reported wording.
