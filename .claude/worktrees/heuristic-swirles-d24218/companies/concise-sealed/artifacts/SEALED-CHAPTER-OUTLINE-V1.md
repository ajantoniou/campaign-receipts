### Layout rule (sell-through)

1. **Margin rail** — Time of day (where known), city/state or venue, audience/speech setting. Makes the reader feel *present*.  
2. **Verbatim block** — Short, italic, sourced.  
3. **Body** — **Not** a teaser clause up front: the **main paragraphs** carry narrative comparison (what happened in office, second-term shifts, reader-led judgment). Hooks cite instruments (votes, orders, datasets), not editorials.

The **5-page retail sample** demonstrates **Part I, Chapter 3, Section A** (“Fair trade, not free trade”) as a full section.
