# Lemon Squeezy — product image (digital book clarity)

Reviewers expect to **see a book or e-reader** before reading policy copy. Upload this hosted asset as the **product image** in the Lemon Squeezy dashboard (product edit screen).

**Image URL (same origin as the live site):**

https://sealed-press.onrender.com/product-images/cover-mockup-v1.jpg

**Steps**

1. Lemon Squeezy → Products → SEALED → edit product.
2. Set **product image / media** — upload from URL or download the JPG and upload manually.
3. Save. Checkout and storefront thumbnails will show the PDF/ePub mockup first.

Square thumbnails: LS may crop center — if the crop feels tight, duplicate the asset locally with padding and replace `public/product-images/cover-mockup-v1.jpg`, then redeploy.
