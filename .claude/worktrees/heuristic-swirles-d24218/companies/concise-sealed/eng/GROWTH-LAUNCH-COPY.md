## 3. Launch email body (full send — 400 words)

**Subject:** (use winning A/B subject from §1)

---

Hi [first name],

In 2015 and 2016, a set of promises was made in plain language — on TV stages, at rallies, in debates watched by tens of millions.

We archived them.

**SEALED: Before the Deals** is not a scorecard we built. It's a time capsule you grade yourself.

Every entry in the book uses the same three-part shape:

1. **The margin rail** — where the promise was made, to whom, and when.
2. **The verbatim quote** — what was actually said, from the primary transcript. We don't paraphrase.
3. **The paper trail** — official records you can look up: laws, votes, executive orders, agency filings.

Then we hand it to you. Five labels: **KEPT, PARTIAL, BROKEN, BLOCKED, MOOT.** You pick one. Your reading, your grade.

**What's inside:**

- 12 chapters across four parts: the campaign record, alliances and force posture, borders and order, and the reader scorecard
- 14 verbatim quotes confirmed against Commission on Presidential Debates transcripts
- Paper trail pointers to debates.org, Congress.gov, USTR, CBP, CMS, FBI UCR — public records, not punditry
- Illustration plates from historical-style archival documents (no faces, no propaganda)
- A one-page delivery snapshot: which promises landed, which didn't, whether you care
