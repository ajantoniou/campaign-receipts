## Scene 2 — What the deal actually did (0:09–0:30)

**VO:** "Plain English first: the deal is called the JCPOA — the Iran nuclear deal. Iran had to keep its nuclear fuel weak, capped at three-point-six-seven percent, while bomb-grade fuel is ninety percent. America lifted sanctions, inspectors could check sites every day, and for three years the cap held even though the deal was never perfect."

**Visual:** Animated bar chart — 3.67% bar (small green) vs 90% bar (tall red, labeled "weapons-grade"). Then a clock animation showing "3 years of compliance."
**Source:** SEALED book line 1519 ("3.67% under the deal"; "60% purity by 2021" after withdrawal).
