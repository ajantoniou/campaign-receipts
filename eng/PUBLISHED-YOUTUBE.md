## Shorts regression watch

**2026-05-23 bug class — silent back-half no-op + AI text garble.**
A `/watch` sweep of all 7 published SEALED shorts found:

- **6 of 7** ended with 15-23 s of the static `145 PROMISES / SEALED2016.COM`
  motion-card playing while the VO was still delivering substantive content
  (May 14 2018, $350B tariffs, 49-51 McCain pause, EO 13983 revocation,
  Gaza-same-day callout, etc.). Only Short 001 Iran (`nu9IlCeGSYc`) — the
  one that used a bespoke driver — was clean.
- **3 of 7** shipped AI-rendered text typos: 002 (`EURAEN` map gibberish),
  004 (`EXESICUTIVE ORDER` + lorem-ipsum body), 005
  (`HAAD COMNAP / UN TARRIT / OF 3ND CCORN` stamp).

**Root cause (1 line):** `findAnchor()` in
`scripts/shorts/produce-short-generic.mjs` treated each `sync_specs`
`startAnchors`/`endAnchors` *array* as a single consecutive-word needle.
Real episode JSONs supply each entry as an independent phrase, so anchor
matching returned `null` for every non-CTA segment, each collapsed to
`MIN_SEG_SEC=3.8 s`, and the un-clamped CTA absorbed 15-23 s — while
`motionFromPng` rendered that CTA card with a broken `zoompan d=frames`
expression that produced a 1080 s clip, truncated to ~30 s frozen on the
first frame.

**Gates that prevent the regression:**

1. `scripts/pipeline/validate-shorts-episode.py` — preflight (before TTS
   spend). Scans every `illustration.prompt` and `broll.prompt` for
   text-trigger tokens (`executive order`, `EO 13\d{3}`, `stamp`,
   `tariff code`, `headline`, `caption`, `document`, dates, dollar
   amounts, vote tallies, …) and exits non-zero with the exact prompt to
   rewrite as Remotion. Wired into `main()` of
   `produce-short-generic.mjs` before any fal.ai call.
2. `scripts/pipeline/ship-checklist.py --mode short` — post-master. Adds
   `shorts_back_half_filled` (FAIL if any single segment > 60 % of
   runtime) and `shorts_no_ai_text_garble` (OCR middle frame of each
   segment, FAIL on AI-garble dictionary). Driver shells this and exits
   non-zero before promoting the master.
3. `produce-short-generic.mjs` — clamps `MIN_CTA_SEC = 2.8` /
   `MAX_CTA_SEC = 5.0`, rewrote `findAnchor` to per-phrase matching, and
   replaced the `motionFromPng` zoom expression with a frame-indexed
   `zoompan d=1` so a 5 s card is actually 5 s long.
